ABOUT CSIJMI


The Computer Society of India was founded in 1965 for the purpose of scientific and educational learning that is directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science, computer engineering, information processing and related arts and sciences. The main objective of CSI is to promote information interchange amongst the specialists and the public. Over the years, it has come a long way to being the largest society associated with IT professionals in India.


The Computer Society of India, Jamia Millia Islamia student chapter, is one such society which has believed in the same since its formulation in the year 2002 under the mentorship of Prof. M.N Doja, Md. Sohrabuddin and other faculty members of the department of Computer Engineering and the active enthusiasm of the students. The society has flourished over the years in its journey from a consortium of 75 odd members to becoming the largest active student society in the university. It has proved to be true to its image of a techno-cultural society enabling students to direct their creative input other than academics, thus expanding their horizon beyond the curriculum.
